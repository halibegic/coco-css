Aspect ratios#

Set a ratio to the element by applying the ratio classes.

1x1
4x3
16x9
21x9
<div class="ratio ratio-1x1">
    <div class="ratio-item">1x1</div>
</div>

<div class="ratio ratio-4x3">
    <div class="ratio-item">4x3</div>
</div>

<div class="ratio ratio-16x9">
    <div class="ratio-item">16x9</div>
</div>

<div class="ratio ratio-21x9">
    <div class="ratio-item">21x9</div>
</div>

Embed#

Insert any YouTube, Vimeo or other iframe/embed video inside the ratio container.

<div class="ratio ratio-16x9">
    <iframe class="ratio-item" src="..." frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
</div>
